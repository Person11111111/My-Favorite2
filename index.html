It is Harry Potter and The Land of Stories.

The Book

Harry Potter is the protagonist of a series of young adult novels by J. K. Rowling. ... Harry, although an ordinary boy in the normal world, has potentially great power as a wizard. In appearance, Harry is a slight, dark-haired boy with a scar on his forehead left from a magical battle that occurred when he was an infant.The Land of Stories tells the tale of twins Alex and Conner. Through the mysterious powers of a cherished book of stories, they leave their world behind and find themselves in a foreign land full of wonder and magic where they come face-to-face with fairy tale characters they grew up reading about.
